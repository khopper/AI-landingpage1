How it works: 4‑week starter sprint

What you’ll have in 4 weeks: hard evidence, a confident next step, and a team that wants to keep going.

Week 0

Intake & Alignment (2–3 hours)

Identify a high‑leverage foothold; align on success metrics and guardrails. Artifacts: Success Brief, data/IT guardrails.

Week 1

Prototype with Your Team

Build a scrappy, safe pilot in a controlled setting using your data context. Artifacts: working demo, risk register.

Week 2

Pilot in the Real Workflow

Limited exposure with real users; capture outcomes and feedback. Artifacts: pilot metrics, change notes.

Week 3

Measure & Prove

Quantify impact (time saved, error reduction, throughput); collect testimonials; document learnings.

Week 4

Recommend Next Step

Go/No‑Go options: expand pilot, productionize, or pivot to a better foothold. Artifacts: Proof Pack (metrics, demo, exec one‑pager), Scale Options.
